Share what helped you during your web3 journey!

I look forward to your contribution (-:


The resources have to meet the following criteria:

  • related to web3/blockchain/DLT
  • free to access (except books)
  • fit in one of the categories
  • no promotional content

In case of a valid submission, you can earn a contributor's POAP. Fill out the contact form below, to suggest a resource. You can also write directly to web3-resources@web.de, contact me on Twitter, or message to web3-resources.eth via dm3 (ENS messaging protocol).

Alternatively, feel free to directly open a pull request on GitHub.

Found a broken link? I'm not always up-to-date, so your message will be appreciated.


Please note: Submissions are reviewed and added at the end of every month.
I might take some time to get back to you.
